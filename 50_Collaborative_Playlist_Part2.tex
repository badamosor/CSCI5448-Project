    \textbf{Title:} Music Playlist Manger
	\section{Summary}
	Sharing playlists of music between a large group of people can be difficult when
	everyone doesn't listen to music through the same service. This will allow people to create
	playlists in a generic format that can then be integrated into the userâ€™s service of choice. This allows people to share their playlists with their friends who can then export it into the streaming service of their choice.
